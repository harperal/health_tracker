Exercise and activity are a vital part of maintaining our overall health. Tracking our fitness and our caloric deficit or overage can have great affects to our overall health. When we are aware of the calories we expend by keeping an activity log we can stay accountable in how we are taking care of our bodies. The below Activity Log allows you to keep track of all the activity you achieve throughout the day. As an added feature there is an additional column to note how the food you have eaten made you feel. Since being active is vital for our health it is important to note the impact of how different activities can affect the way we feel.

How to add exercise to the log?

# Steps
1 Navigate below the Fitness Log
2 Fill in the designated text boxes to add exercise, length, calories and an optional description
3 Click the Add button to the right of the form to add to Fitness Log
4 Should you need to search for the amount of calories burned during exercise, use the search bar below.
